# Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? This page covers the most common questions we hear from clients. If your question isn't answered here, send Korneel a message on Slack and we'll get back to you.

## Getting Support

### How do I get support?

Message Korneel directly on Slack. That's our primary communication channel for everything website-related. If you're on the Care Plan, you get priority support with a response within 24 hours on business days. For Hosting-only clients, we respond within 48 hours.

### What's the fastest way to get help?

Slack is always the fastest route. Be specific about what you need -- include the page name, section, and exactly what's going on. Screenshots are incredibly helpful if something looks off. The more context you give, the faster we can help.

### Do you offer phone or email support?

Our primary support channel is Slack because it keeps everything organized and trackable. If something is urgent or complex enough to warrant a call, we'll schedule one. But for most questions and requests, Slack is faster for both of us.

### What are your business hours?

We're generally available Monday through Friday, European business hours (CET/CEST). We don't monitor Slack on weekends or public holidays, but we'll pick up any messages first thing on the next business day.

## Website Editing

### Can I edit my own website?

Yes, absolutely. You have full access to Sanity CMS, which is where all your website content lives. You can change text, swap images, add blog posts, and more -- all from a user-friendly interface in your browser. Check out our [Logging Into Sanity](/website-management/logging-into-sanity) guide to get started.

### Can I break my website by editing in Sanity?

It's very hard to break things. Sanity has built-in validation that prevents you from saving content that doesn't meet the required format. It also keeps a full version history, so if something does go wrong, we can always roll back to a previous version. Edit with confidence.

### Do I need any technical skills to edit my site?

Not at all. If you can use a word processor, you can use Sanity. It's designed for people who aren't technical. You type text, upload images, and hit publish. We've set everything up so the fields are clearly labeled and intuitive.

### Can I preview changes before they go live?

Yes. Sanity has a preview mode that lets you see your changes before publishing. The exact setup depends on your website, but most of our sites include a preview button that shows you what the live page will look like with your edits.

## Pages and Content

### What if I need a new page?

Reach out on Slack and describe what you need. Simple pages (like a new landing page with standard sections) can sometimes be added as part of your Care Plan hours. More complex pages that require custom design or new functionality are handled as a separate project with a clear quote.

### Can I add a blog?

If your website already has a blog section, you can add new posts yourself directly in Sanity. Just create a new blog post, add your content, and publish. If your site doesn't have a blog section yet, we can add that functionality as a small project -- usually just a few hours of work.

### Can I reorder pages or sections on my site?

In most cases, yes. Sanity allows you to reorder sections within a page by dragging and dropping. Reordering pages in the navigation may require a small configuration change -- just ask on Slack and we'll handle it.

### How do I update images on my website?

You can upload new images directly in Sanity. Navigate to the page, find the image field, and upload your new file. Sanity automatically optimizes images for web performance. For details, check out our [Updating Images](/website-management/updating-images) guide.

<Callout kind="tip">
When uploading images, use the highest quality version you have. Our system handles compression and optimization automatically, so you don't need to resize images before uploading.
</Callout>

## Plans and Billing

### How do I cancel or change my plan?

Message Korneel on Slack. There are no long-term contracts and no cancellation fees. You can cancel anytime, and the change takes effect at the end of your current billing period. Upgrading from Hosting to Care Plan (or vice versa) is just as simple.

### What's the difference between the Hosting Plan and Care Plan?

The **Hosting Plan** (EUR 29/month) covers hosting, SSL, uptime monitoring, and basic Slack support. The **Care Plan** (EUR 99/month) includes everything in the Hosting Plan plus content updates, monthly performance reports, priority support, and proactive monitoring. See our [full comparison](/hosting-care-plan/care-plan) for details.

### Do I own my website?

Yes, 100%. The code, the design, the content -- it's all yours. We built it for you and you own it completely. If you ever want to take your website elsewhere, we'll hand over everything: the codebase, the Sanity project, the design files. No strings attached.

### What happens if I stop the hosting plan?

Your website goes offline at the end of your current billing period. But the code and content remain yours. We can help you migrate to another hosting provider if you want to keep your site running elsewhere. Your Sanity content stays intact too.

### Can I pay annually?

For the Hosting Plan, yes. The annual option is EUR 290/year (compared to EUR 348 if you pay monthly), saving you roughly EUR 58. Just let us know on Slack if you'd like to switch to annual billing.

### How does billing work?

We send invoices monthly (or annually if you've chosen that option). Payment is typically handled via bank transfer or a payment link we send with the invoice. If you have a preferred payment method, let us know and we'll try to accommodate it.

## Technical

### What tech stack is my website built on?

Your website uses three core technologies. **Next.js** is the framework that powers your website (it makes your site fast and SEO-friendly). **Sanity CMS** is where you edit your content. **Vercel** is where your site is hosted. For a deeper explanation, see [Your Website Overview](/getting-started/your-website-overview).

### Is my website secure?

Yes. Vercel provides automatic SSL encryption (HTTPS), DDoS protection, and enterprise-grade infrastructure security. We follow security best practices in our code, and Sanity CMS has its own security measures including authentication and access controls.

### Is my website GDPR compliant?

We take privacy seriously. By default, we use PostHog for analytics, which is EU-hosted and cookieless -- meaning no cookie consent banner is needed for analytics tracking. If you're on a Growth or Signature plan with GA4 and Meta Pixel, we include a cookie consent banner that lets visitors opt in or out. For the privacy policy and legal compliance of your specific business, we recommend consulting with a legal professional.

### How fast is my website?

Very fast. Next.js and Vercel are optimized for performance, and we follow best practices for image optimization, code splitting, and caching. Most of our websites score 90+ on Google's PageSpeed Insights. If you're curious about your specific score, just ask and we'll run a test.

### Will my website work on mobile?

Absolutely. Every website we build is fully responsive, meaning it automatically adapts to look great on phones, tablets, and desktops. We test on multiple devices and screen sizes during the build process.

### Can I connect my own domain?

Your website is already connected to your domain. If you want to change your domain or add an additional one, let us know on Slack. We handle all DNS configuration and make sure everything is properly connected with SSL.

## Analytics and Tracking

### What analytics do I get?

Every website we build includes PostHog analytics, which tracks page views, clicks, form submissions, and more -- all without cookies. Growth and Signature clients also get Google Analytics 4 and Meta Pixel. For more details, see our [Analytics Overview](/analytics/overview).

### Can I see my analytics data?

Yes. Care Plan clients receive a monthly report with key highlights. You can also request direct access to your PostHog dashboard or your GA4 dashboard (if applicable). Just ask on Slack and we'll set it up.

### Why are my analytics numbers different across platforms?

PostHog and Google Analytics use different tracking methods. PostHog is cookieless and captures all visitors, while GA4 only tracks visitors who accept cookies. This means PostHog usually shows higher numbers. Both are accurate -- they're just measuring differently. See our [Analytics Overview](/analytics/overview) for more context.

## Growth Tools

### What are Growth Tools?

Growth Tools are marketing and business tools powered by GoHighLevel that help you manage your online presence beyond just your website. They include features like a social media planner, forms and surveys, email marketing, calendar and booking, a CRM pipeline, review management, and an AI chatbot. See the [Growth Tools](/growth-tools/social-planner/overview) section for details on each tool.

### Can I add Growth Tools to my existing plan?

Yes. Growth Tools are available as an add-on to any plan. The setup is a one-time fee of EUR 500, plus EUR 30/month for ongoing access. Reach out on Slack and we'll walk you through what's included and get you set up.

### Do I need Growth Tools?

Not necessarily. Your website works perfectly fine without them. Growth Tools are for clients who want an all-in-one platform for managing their marketing, leads, and client communication. If you're already using separate tools for email marketing, social scheduling, and booking, Growth Tools can consolidate everything into one place.

### Can I use Growth Tools without a website from TransformWebsites?

Growth Tools are designed to work alongside your TransformWebsites website, but they can function on their own too. If you're interested, reach out on Slack and we'll discuss what makes sense for your situation.
