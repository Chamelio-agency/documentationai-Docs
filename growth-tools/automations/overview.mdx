# Automations Overview

Imagine having a virtual assistant who never sleeps, never forgets, and handles all the repetitive tasks in your business without you lifting a finger. That's exactly what automations do.

At their core, automations are simple "if this, then that" rules that run in the background, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. When something happens (like a form submission), your system automatically does something in response (like sending a confirmation email). No manual work required.

---

## How Automations Work

Think of automations like a chain of dominoes. One event knocks over the next, and so on. Here's the basic idea:

1. **Something happens** (a trigger) -- someone fills out your contact form, books a session, or signs up for your newsletter.
2. **Your system responds** (an action) -- it sends an email, creates a task, adds a tag, or moves them through your pipeline.
3. **It all happens instantly**, without you touching a thing.

That's it. No coding, no complicated setup. Just smart rules working behind the scenes.

---

## Real-World Examples for Wellness Businesses

Here are some automations you'll probably recognize from your own setup:

### Someone fills out your contact form
They immediately get a warm welcome email with your introduction and what to expect next. No more "I'll get back to you when I have a moment" -- it happens in seconds.

### Someone books a discovery call
They receive a confirmation email with the call details, a calendar invite, and a short note about how to prepare. Then, 24 hours before the call, they get a friendly reminder so they don't forget.

### After a retreat or session ends
A few days later, your client receives a thoughtful email thanking them for the experience and gently asking them to leave a Google review. You didn't have to remember. You didn't have to write the email. It just happened.

### Someone signs up for your newsletter
They don't just get silence. They enter a nurture sequence -- a series of carefully timed emails that introduce your services, share your story, and build trust over the course of a couple of weeks.

## Why This Matters for Your Business

If you're a yoga teacher, retreat leader, therapist, or wellness coach, you know this feeling: you're in the middle of a session, and somewhere in the back of your mind you're thinking, "Did I follow up with that lead from yesterday?"

Automations remove that mental load entirely.

Here's what they do for you in practice:

- **No lead falls through the cracks.** Every person who reaches out gets a response, even if you're in a session, on a retreat, or simply taking a day off.
- **You look professional and organized.** Instant confirmations and timely follow-ups make your business feel polished and trustworthy.
- **You save hours every week.** The time you used to spend writing individual emails, sending reminders, and following up manually? That's now handled for you.
- **You can focus on what you do best.** Teaching, coaching, healing -- not admin work.

## A Few Things to Keep in Mind

**Automations are always-on.** Once they're active, they run every time the trigger condition is met. If someone fills out your form at 3 AM on a Sunday, they'll still get a response immediately.

**They're customizable.** The emails, timing, and logic can all be adjusted. Want to change the wording of your booking confirmation? Want to add an extra follow-up email? Just let us know.

**They work together.** Automations don't exist in isolation. Your form submission automation might tag a contact, which triggers another automation that adds them to a nurture sequence. It's a connected system.
