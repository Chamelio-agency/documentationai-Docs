# Course Offers, Bundles & Upsells

When you [create a course](/growth-tools/courses/creating-courses), an offer is automatically created with it. But offers are more flexible than just "one course, one price." You can create bundles that include multiple courses, set up discount codes, and even upsell additional courses to people who've already purchased.

## Editing an offer

Click **Edit** on any offer to change its details. [Watch this step (0:20)](https://www.loom.com/share/c61dabf2ff8e45c5ad0bf6394019deab?t=20)

<Image
  src="https://loom.com/i/536b736267734ac8898ef59fa7f6aa55?workflows_screenshot=true"
  alt="Offer editor showing title, description, and product connection"
  width="800"
  height="450"
/>

You can update:
- **Title** — the name clients see (e.g., "8-Week Mindfulness Programme" or "Full Transformation Bundle")
- **Description** — what's included and why it's valuable
- **Connected product(s)** — which course(s) this offer gives access to

### Advanced settings

Under the **Advanced** tab, you'll find options for tracking codes and custom styling. Most clients won't need these. [Watch this step (0:39)](https://www.loom.com/share/c61dabf2ff8e45c5ad0bf6394019deab?t=39)

You can also toggle the offer between **Published** (live and purchasable) and **Draft** (hidden from clients).

## Pricing and discounts

On the right side of the offer editor, you'll find the pricing settings. [Watch this step (1:02)](https://www.loom.com/share/c61dabf2ff8e45c5ad0bf6394019deab?t=62)

- **Price** — set or change the price for this offer
- **Discount codes** — enable this to let clients enter promo codes at checkout (you create these in [Payments → Coupons](/growth-tools/payments/products-and-links))

### Setting access dates

You can limit when people can access the course — for example, only available from a specific start date, or access expires after a certain number of days. [Watch this step (1:18)](https://www.loom.com/share/c61dabf2ff8e45c5ad0bf6394019deab?t=78)

<Callout kind="info">
  When someone purchases an offer, they automatically get access to the course(s) included — no manual action needed from your side. A welcome email with login details is sent automatically if you have it enabled in your [client portal settings](/growth-tools/courses/creating-courses).

<Callout kind="alert">
  Make sure your offer is set to **Published** before sharing the link. Only published offers can be purchased. If it's still in Draft mode, the checkout page won't work for clients.
</Callout>

## Creating bundle offers

A bundle lets you package multiple courses together into a single offer at a special price. [Watch this step (2:52)](https://www.loom.com/share/c61dabf2ff8e45c5ad0bf6394019deab?t=172)

<Image
  src="https://loom.com/i/31495dcebd8b433fb377678c6396fdce?workflows_screenshot=true"
  alt="A bundle offer showing multiple courses included in one package"
  width="800"
  height="450"
/>

For example, a "Complete Transformation Package" might include:
- Your 8-Week Mindfulness Programme
- Your Yoga for Beginners course
- Your Nutrition Fundamentals guide

When someone purchases the bundle, they get access to all three courses at once.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Bundles are a great way to increase the average value of each sale. Price the bundle at a discount compared to buying each course individually — for example, if the three courses cost EUR 297 individually, offer the bundle at EUR 197. Clients feel they're getting a deal, and you earn more per transaction.
</Callout>

## Setting up upsells

Upsells let you suggest additional courses to people who've already bought something. There are two types: [Watch this step (3:18)](https://www.loom.com/share/c61dabf2ff8e45c5ad0bf6394019deab?t=198)

<Image
  src="https://loom.com/i/9cf9302674af41909c26eb91ae62c968?workflows_screenshot=true"
  alt="Upsell options showing one-click upsell and in-app upsell"
  width="800"
  height="450"
/>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="One-click upsell" icon="zap">
    This appears right after someone completes their purchase on the checkout page. They see an offer for an additional course and can buy it with a single click — no need to re-enter payment details.

   **Example:** Someone buys your Mindfulness Programme and immediately sees: "Add Yoga for Beginners for just EUR 47" with a one-click buy button.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="In-app upsell" icon="book-open">
    This appears inside the client portal when someone is browsing their courses. In their "My Library" section, they see locked courses they haven't purchased yet — with a purchase button right there.

   **Example:** A client who bought your Mindfulness Programme sees your Nutrition course displayed as a suggested purchase in their library. When they're ready, they can buy it without leaving the portal.

   You can attach multiple in-app upsells to the same offer, and each one can be fully customised — different styling, descriptions, and instructor info per upsell. Once someone purchases an upsell, it disappears from the locked list and becomes part of their library.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Connecting an upsell

To set up an upsell, choose which offer you want to suggest and connect it. [Watch this step (4:00)](https://www.loom.com/share/c61dabf2ff8e45c5ad0bf6394019deab?t=240)

<Image
  src="https://loom.com/i/69cf292f19c540acb366ff9dcf58943b?workflows_screenshot=true"
  alt="Connecting an upsell to an offer by selecting which course to suggest"
  width="800"
  height="450"
/>

Select the offer you want to promote, and the system handles the rest — showing it at the right moment to the right people.

<Callout kind="tip">
  For in-app upsells, you can customise the upsell page with your own colours, description, curriculum preview, and instructor bio. This makes the upsell feel like a natural part of the course experience rather than an ad. Access the visual editor by clicking the palette icon next to the upsell in your offer settings.
</Callout>

## Offer ideas for wellness businesses

| Offer type | Example | Strategy |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Single course | "8-Week Mindfulness Programme" — EUR 197 | Your core offering |
| Bundle | "Complete Transformation" — EUR 297 (3 courses) | Higher value, perceived discount |
| Free lead magnet | "3-Day Meditation Starter" — Free | Capture leads, build trust |
| Time-limited access | "Summer Retreat Prep" — EUR 47, 30-day access | Creates urgency |
| One-click upsell | After buying Mindfulness → offer Yoga for EUR 47 | Increase per-sale value |
| In-app upsell | Show Nutrition course in their library | Passive, low-pressure sales |
