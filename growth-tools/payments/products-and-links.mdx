# Products, Payment Links & Coupons

Before you can accept payments, you need to set up what you're selling. This guide walks you through creating products, generating payment links you can share anywhere, and setting up coupon codes for discounts.

## Creating a product

Every service or offer you sell needs to exist as a product first. Whether it's a coaching session, a retreat spot, a course, or a membership — create it here and then you can sell it through payment links, forms, or your booking system.

<Callout kind="tip">
  You can add multiple prices to the same product. For example, a "Coaching Package" could have a EUR 150 single-session option and a EUR 97/month recurring option — both under one product.
</Callout>

## Creating a payment link

A payment link is a URL you can send to anyone — via email, WhatsApp, Instagram, text message, or even printed on a flyer. When they click it, they see your product, fill in their details, and pay. Simple as that. [Watch this step (4:30)](https://www.loom.com/share/256a8fa86e2943cca9c6572a19ea4ccf?t=270)

<Image
  src="https://loom.com/i/52b70e238e3c43e2bf279aa2f081f45a?workflows_screenshot=true"
  alt="The payment link creation page showing product selection and customisation options"
  width="800"
  height="450"
/>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Payment Links and click Create Link" icon="link" title-type="p">
    Navigate to **Payments → Payment Links** and click **Create Link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the product" icon="tag" title-type="p">
    Choose which product(s) the client will pay for. You can add multiple products to a single link — for example, a retreat spot plus a pre-retreat coaching call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure options" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    You can require a phone number, add your branding (colours and logo), include terms and conditions, and customise the button text. [Watch this step (5:16)](https://www.loom.com/share/256a8fa86e2943cca9c6572a19ea4ccf?t=316)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and copy the link" icon="copy" title-type="p">
    Save the payment link and copy the URL. You can now share it anywhere.

   <Image
     src="https://loom.com/i/36d54db5c4784c4eb24d6d0f95bc333f?workflows_screenshot=true"
     alt="The saved payment link ready to copy and share"
     width="800"
     height="450"
   />
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Where to share your payment links

- **Email** — include it in a follow-up email after a discovery call
- **WhatsApp or text** — send it directly in a conversation
- **Instagram bio** — perfect for linking to your signature offer
- **Your website** — we can add it as a button on any page
- **In person** — share as a QR code at events or on printed materials

<Callout kind="tip">
  You can also send personalised payment links to multiple contacts at once — their name and email will be pre-filled so they just need to enter payment details. Great for sending a retreat deposit link to a group.
</Callout>

<Callout kind="info">
  Payment links support both one-time and recurring products. If you add a subscription product, the client will be charged automatically each month after their first payment.
</Callout>

## Creating coupons

Coupons are discount codes your clients enter at checkout. They're great for early-bird pricing, referral discounts, or special promotions. [Watch this step (5:28)](https://www.loom.com/share/256a8fa86e2943cca9c6572a19ea4ccf?t=328)

<Image
  src="https://loom.com/i/b6f36fb38ff94664a337926bb2f47f0d?workflows_screenshot=true"
  alt="The coupon creation form with fields for name, code, type, and validity dates"
  width="800"
  height="450"
/>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Coupons and click Create" icon="plus-circle" title-type="p">
    Navigate to **Payments → Coupons** and click **Create Coupon**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the coupon name and code" icon="tag" title-type="p">
    The **name** is for your reference (e.g., "Early Bird 2025"). The **code** is what clients type at checkout (e.g., "EARLYBIRD20"). Keep codes short, memorable, and easy to type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the discount type" icon="percent" title-type="p">
    - **Percentage off** — e.g., 20% off the total
    - **Fixed amount off** — e.g., EUR 50 off

   Set when the coupon starts and when it expires.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the rules" icon="shield" title-type="p">
    You can limit how the coupon is used. [Watch this step (6:13)](https://www.loom.com/share/256a8fa86e2943cca9c6572a19ea4ccf?t=373)

   <Image
     src="https://loom.com/i/8eb82d31f7cd4c63ba216c476439f780?workflows_screenshot=true"
     alt="Coupon rules settings showing usage limits and product restrictions"
     width="800"
     height="450"
   />

   - **Total number of uses** — limit how many times the coupon can be used in total
   - **Limit to specific products** — only apply to certain products (e.g., only the retreat, not coaching sessions)
   - **Once per customer** — prevent the same person from using it twice
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="alert">
  Coupons work on payment links and order forms, but **not on invoices**. Keep this in mind when choosing how to collect payment for a discounted offer.
</Callout>

### Coupon ideas for wellness businesses

| Coupon | Code | Use case |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Early-bird retreat discount | EARLYBIRD20 | 20% off for the first 10 sign-ups |
| Referral reward | FRIEND50 | EUR 50 off when referred by an existing client |
| Returning client | WELCOME-BACK | 15% off for past retreat guests |
| Holiday special | NEWYEAR25 | 25% off coaching packages in January |
| Group booking | GROUP10 | 10% off when 3+ people book together |
