# Setting Up Your Brand Board

A brand board is your pre-saved set of colours, fonts, and brand voice. Set it up once, and every email you create will have your branding available at your fingertips — no more hunting for hex codes or guessing which shade of blue you used last time.

## What's in a brand board

Your brand board stores three things: [Watch this step (0:00)](https://www.loom.com/share/e3af895884864e3cb37e75686949363f?t=0)

<Image
  src="https://loom.com/i/9d719dd45afa4a5ca0da018f1acdd2e7?workflows_screenshot=true"
  alt="Brand board overview showing colour, typography, and brand voice sections"
  width="800"
  height="450"
/>

- **Brand colours** — your primary colour, secondary colour, accent colours, background colours
- **Typography** — your heading font, body text font, and sizes
- **Brand voice** — a description of how your brand sounds (warm and inviting? professional and calm? energetic and bold?)

## Setting it up

Navigate to your brand board settings and fill in each section. [Watch this step (0:15)](https://www.loom.com/share/e3af895884864e3cb37e75686949363f?t=15)

<Image
  src="https://loom.com/i/c96dc94f46124e028f4c6dc878425cc7?workflows_screenshot=true"
  alt="Brand board setup showing colour picker and typography selection"
  width="800"
  height="450"
/>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your brand colours" icon="palette" title-type="p">
    Add the colours you use in your branding. If you're not sure what your exact colours are, check your website or ask Korneel — we have them on file from when we built your site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your fonts" icon="type" title-type="p">
    Select the fonts that match your brand. If you want to use the same fonts as your website, we can tell you which ones they are.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define your brand voice" icon="mic" title-type="p">
    Describe how your brand communicates. For example: "Warm, approachable, and knowledgeable. We speak to our clients like a trusted friend who happens to be an expert in wellness."
  </Step>
</Steps>
