# Hosting Plan

Your website needs a home on the internet, and that's exactly what our Hosting Plan provides. For EUR 29 per month, we take care of the technical infrastructure so your website stays fast, secure, and online around the clock.

Think of it like renting a premium spot on the internet. You don't need to worry about servers, security certificates, or uptime. We handle all of that for you.

## What's Included

Here's everything that comes with your Hosting Plan.

### Vercel Hosting on a Global CDN

Your website is hosted on Vercel, one of the most reliable hosting platforms available. Vercel uses a global Content Delivery Network (CDN), which means your site is served from data centers around the world. When someone in Bali visits your retreat website, they get a fast experience just like someone browsing from Brussels.

This is the same infrastructure used by companies like Nike, Uber, and The Washington Post. Your wellness business gets enterprise-level performance at a fraction of the cost.

### Automatic SSL Certificate (HTTPS)

You know the little padlock icon in your browser's address bar? That's SSL, and it means the connection between your visitor and your website is encrypted and secure. We set this up automatically and renew it for you. You never have to think about it.

SSL isn't just about security. Google also uses it as a ranking factor, so having HTTPS helps your search visibility too.

### Uptime Monitoring

We actively monitor your website to make sure it stays online. If your site goes down for any reason, we get alerted immediately and jump on it. Most issues are resolved before you even notice something happened.

You shouldn't have to sit there refreshing your own website to check if it's working. That's our job.

### Basic Support via Slack

Got a question about your hosting? Something look off on your site? Send a message on Slack and we'll get back to you within 48 hours on business days. For straightforward hosting questions, it's usually much faster than that.

<Callout kind="info">
Your hosting is month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime by sending a message on Slack. We believe in earning your business every month, not locking you in.
</Callout>

## What's NOT Included

The Hosting Plan keeps your website running and secure. It doesn't include hands-on content work. Here's what falls outside the plan.

- **Content updates** (text changes, image swaps, adding new sections)
- **Design changes** (new layouts, styling updates, visual tweaks)
- **New pages** (building additional pages for your site)
- **New features** (adding booking forms, integrations, or new functionality)

These are handled either as one-off projects (just reach out on Slack for a quote) or as part of our Care Plan, which includes content updates and much more.

<Callout kind="tip">
If you find yourself regularly needing text updates, image changes, or small tweaks, the Care Plan at EUR 99/month is probably a better fit. It includes up to about 2 hours of content work per month, plus priority support and monthly performance reports.
</Callout>

## Who Is This For?

The Hosting Plan is a great fit if you:

- **Are comfortable editing your own content in Sanity.** You know how to log in, change text, swap images, and publish updates yourself. (If you're not sure how, check out our [website management guides](/website-management/logging-into-sanity).)
- **Don't need frequent changes.** Your website is mostly set-and-forget, with occasional updates you handle on your own.
- **Want reliable hosting without the extras.** You want your site to be fast, secure, and online, but you don't need us actively managing content for you.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What happens if my site goes down?**
We get alerted automatically and work to resolve it as quickly as possible. Most hosting issues are on Vercel's side and are resolved within minutes. If it's something specific to your site, we'll investigate and fix it.

**Can I upgrade to the Care Plan later?**
Absolutely. Just message Korneel on Slack and we'll switch you over. The change takes effect on your next billing cycle.

**What happens if I cancel?**
Your site goes offline at the end of your current billing period. But the code and content are yours. We'll help you migrate everything to another host if you want.

**Do I own my website?**
Yes, 100%. The code, the design, the content -- it's all yours. We're just hosting and supporting it. If you ever want to take it elsewhere, we'll hand over everything you need.
