# Requesting Changes

Whether you're on the Care Plan or just need a one-off update, the process for requesting website changes is straightforward. Send us a message on Slack, and we'll take it from there.

This guide will help you write effective change requests so we can get things done quickly and accurately. The better your request, the faster the turnaround.

## How the Process Works

<Steps>
<Step title="Send your request on Slack" icon="message-circle" title-type="p">
Describe what you'd like changed. Include the page name, the specific section, and exactly what you want it to say or look like. Attach any images, files, or links directly in the message.
</Step>

<Step title="We confirm scope and timeline" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
We'll review your request and reply with a confirmation. For quick content updates, we'll let you know when to expect it. If it's a bigger request, we'll discuss scope and give you a clear timeline.
</Step>

<Step title="We make the changes" icon="code" title-type="p">
Our team handles the edits, from updating content in Sanity to adjusting code if needed. You don't need to log in or do anything on your end.
</Step>

<Step title="You review and approve" icon="eye" title-type="p">
For significant changes, we'll share a preview link so you can see exactly how things look before they go live. For small text edits, we usually publish directly unless you tell us otherwise.
</Step>

<Step title="Changes go live" icon="globe" title-type="p">
Once everything looks good, the updates are published to your live website. We'll send a quick confirmation in Slack so you know it's done.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Writing a Great Change Request

The single biggest thing you can do to speed up the process is being specific. Here's the difference between a vague request and a clear one.

### Less Helpful

> "Can you update the homepage?"

This tells us something needs to change, but we don't know what. We'll have to ask follow-up questions, which adds a day or two to the turnaround.

### Much Better

> "On the homepage hero section, change the headline from 'Find Your Inner Peace' to 'Transform Your Life Through Breathwork.' Also swap the hero image -- new image attached."

This tells us exactly what to change, where to change it, and gives us everything we need to get it done in one go.

### The Perfect Change Request Includes:

1. **Which page** -- "homepage," "about page," "retreat page," etc.
2. **Which section** -- "the hero section," "the testimonials area," "the footer"
3. **What to change** -- the exact new text, the new image, the updated link
4. **Any attached files** -- new images, logos, PDFs, or documents

<Callout kind="tip">
Batching multiple small changes into one request is more efficient than sending them one by one. If you have three small edits, send them all in a single Slack message. This helps us plan the work and often means everything gets done faster.
</Callout>

## What's Included in Your Care Plan

If you're on the Care Plan (EUR 99/month), you get approximately 2 hours of content work per month. Here's what typically falls within that scope.

### Content Updates (Included)

These are changes to things that already exist on your website:

- **Text edits** -- changing headlines, paragraphs, bios, descriptions, dates, or prices
- **Image swaps** -- replacing photos or graphics anywhere on your site
- **Adding content to existing sections** -- a new testimonial, a new FAQ question, an additional team member
- **Blog posts** -- formatting and publishing blog posts you've written
- **Link updates** -- changing where buttons or menu items point to
- **Small layout tweaks** -- reordering sections, adjusting spacing, minor visual changes
- **SEO updates** -- updating page titles, meta descriptions, or alt text on images

### New Projects (Separate Quote)

These involve creating something that doesn't exist yet:

- **New pages** -- designing and building a page from scratch (like a new retreat page or landing page)
- **Section redesigns** -- completely rethinking how a section looks and works
- **New features** -- adding booking functionality, payment processing, member areas, or integrations
- **Major structural changes** -- reorganizing your site navigation or completely restructuring page layouts
- **Branding updates** -- new logo, new color scheme, new typography across the site

<Callout kind="info">
Not sure whether your request is an update or a project? Just send it over. We'll let you know honestly, and if it's a project, we'll give you a clear quote before we start any work. There are never any surprise charges.
</Callout>

## Image Guidelines

When sending us images for your website, here are a few tips to help things go smoothly.

### File Format
- **JPEG** or **PNG** are ideal for photos
- **PNG** is best for graphics with transparent backgrounds
- **SVG** works great for logos and icons

### Image Size
- **Hero images** (full-width banners): at least 1920px wide
- **Content images** (in-page photos): at least 1200px wide
- **Profile photos or headshots**: at least 800px wide
- Don't worry about making them smaller -- our system automatically optimizes images for fast loading

### Quality
- Use the highest quality version you have. We'll handle compression.
- Avoid screenshots of images or photos pulled from social media -- they're usually too low resolution.
- Professional photography always looks better than stock photos, but we can work with both.

<Callout kind="tip">
If you're not sure about an image, send it anyway. We'll let you know if the quality or size isn't quite right, and we can suggest alternatives.
</Callout>

## Examples of Real Requests

Here are some examples of the kind of change requests we handle regularly. These should give you an idea of what to send.

**Quick text edit:**
> "On the About page, third paragraph, change 'I've been teaching yoga for 10 years' to 'I've been teaching yoga for 12 years.'"

**Image swap with new content:**
> "Replace the main photo on the Retreats page with the attached image. Also update the retreat dates to 'June 15-22, 2026' and change the price to 'EUR 1,850 per person.'"

**New blog post:**
> "Please publish this blog post (attached as a Word doc). Use the attached photo as the featured image. Suggested title: 'Why Breathwork Changed Everything for Me.' Category: Wellness Tips."

**Multiple small updates:**
> "A few updates for this month:
> 1. Homepage: change the CTA button text from 'Learn More' to 'Book a Free Call'
> 2. Footer: update the phone number to +32 xxx xxx xxx
> 3. About page: add the new certification logo (attached) to the credentials section"

## Not on the Care Plan?

You can still request changes as one-off projects. Just reach out on Slack and describe what you need. We'll review your request and send you a quote with a clear scope and timeline.

One-off changes are billed based on the time required. Small updates might be as little as EUR 50-100, while bigger projects are quoted individually.

If you find yourself requesting changes regularly, the Care Plan at EUR 99/month is usually more cost-effective and comes with the added benefits of monthly reports and priority support.
