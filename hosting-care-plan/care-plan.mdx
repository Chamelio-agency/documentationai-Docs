# Care Plan

The Care Plan is for wellness entrepreneurs who want to focus on their clients, not their website. For EUR 99 per month, we take care of your website so you don't have to think about it.

You get everything in the Hosting Plan, plus content updates, monthly performance reports, priority support, and proactive monitoring. It's like having a dedicated web team on call, without the overhead of hiring one.

## What's Included

### Everything in the Hosting Plan

You still get all the basics: Vercel hosting on a global CDN, automatic SSL certificates, uptime monitoring, and Slack support. The Care Plan builds on top of that foundation.

### Content Updates (Up to ~2 Hours per Month)

Need to change a headline? Swap out a photo? Add a new testimonial? Update your retreat dates? Just send us a message on Slack and we'll handle it.

Your Care Plan includes approximately 2 hours of content work per month. That covers a surprising amount of ground. Here's what typically fits within that time:

- **Text changes** -- updating headlines, descriptions, bios, or any written content
- **Image swaps** -- replacing photos on your homepage, about page, or anywhere else
- **Adding new sections** -- inserting a testimonial block, a new FAQ, or an additional content section
- **Blog posts** -- we can publish blog posts you've written, including formatting and images
- **Small layout tweaks** -- adjusting spacing, reordering sections, or minor styling changes
- **Link updates** -- changing buttons, menu items, or footer links

### Monthly Performance Report

Around the first week of each month, you'll receive a report in Slack with key metrics about how your website is performing. We break the numbers down in plain language so you can actually understand what's going on.

The report covers visitors, page views, top pages, traffic sources, and conversions. No jargon, no confusing charts. Just the numbers that matter and what they mean for your business.

Learn more about how to read your report in our [Monthly Reports](/hosting-care-plan/monthly-reports) guide.

### Priority Slack Support

Care Plan clients get priority support with a response time within 24 hours on business days. In practice, we often respond the same day, especially for straightforward requests.

You also get priority for scheduling project work. If you need something bigger done (like a new page or feature), Care Plan clients go to the front of the queue.

### Proactive Monitoring

We don't just wait for things to break. We actively keep an eye on your site's health, speed, and security. This includes:

- **Performance checks** -- making sure your site loads quickly on desktop and mobile
- **Security monitoring** -- keeping your site safe and up to date
- **Broken link detection** -- catching dead links before your visitors do
- **Core Web Vitals tracking** -- monitoring the metrics Google uses to evaluate your site experience

If we spot something that needs attention, we'll fix it proactively or let you know what's going on. You don't have to ask.

## What Counts as a "Content Update" vs. a "New Project"?

This is one of the most common questions we get, and it's a fair one. Here's a simple way to think about it.

### Content Updates (Included in Care Plan)

A content update is a change to something that already exists on your website. You're modifying existing content or structure, not building something new from scratch.

- Changing the headline on your homepage
- Swapping out the hero image
- Updating your retreat dates or pricing
- Adding a new testimonial to an existing testimonials section
- Publishing a blog post you've written
- Updating your bio or team page
- Fixing a typo (it happens to all of us)

### New Projects (Separate Quote)

A new project involves building something that doesn't exist yet. This requires design thinking, development work, or significant new functionality.

- Building a brand new page from scratch
- Redesigning an entire section of your website
- Adding a booking system or payment integration
- Creating a new interactive feature
- Building a members-only area
- Major structural changes to your site's layout

<Callout kind="info">
Not sure whether your request is an update or a project? Just send it over on Slack. We'll let you know, and if it's a project, we'll give you a clear quote before any work begins. No surprises.
</Callout>

### What If I Need More Than 2 Hours?

Some months you might have more updates than usual. That's fine. If a request goes slightly over the 2-hour mark, we won't nickel-and-dime you. We look at usage over time, not on a stopwatch.

If you consistently need more than 2 hours of work per month, we'll have a conversation about what makes sense. We might suggest batching requests, upgrading your plan, or handling the extra work as a small project.

The Care Plan is designed for wellness entrepreneurs who:
