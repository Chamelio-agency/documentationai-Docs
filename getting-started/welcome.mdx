# Welcome to the TransformWebsites Help Center

You made a great choice investing in a professional website for your wellness business. This help center is here to make sure you feel confident managing it, understanding how it works, and knowing exactly where to go when you need help.

Whether you're looking to update a page, add a blog post, or just figure out what "Vercel" means, you're in the right place.

## How to Use This Help Center

There are a few ways to find what you need here.

**Browse by category.** Use the sidebar on the left to explore articles grouped by topic. We've organized everything so you can find answers logically, from getting started basics to advanced website management.

**Search for it.** Use the search bar at the top of the page. Type a few keywords describing what you're looking for, and we'll surface the most relevant articles. Try things like "edit page," "add blog post," or "image sizes."

**Ask the AI assistant.** If you're not sure where to look, use the AI chatbot built into this help center. Just type your question in natural language and it will point you to the right article or give you a direct answer based on our documentation.

<Callout kind="tip">
The AI assistant works best when you ask specific questions. Instead of "help with my website," try "how do I change the hero image on my homepage?" The more specific you are, the better the answer.
</Callout>

## Need Help Beyond the Docs?

This help center covers the most common questions and tasks. But sometimes you need a human.

If you can't find what you're looking for here, the fastest way to get help is to message Korneel directly on Slack. That's our primary communication channel, and you'll typically get a response within 24 hours on business days.

## A Quick Note About Loom Videos

Throughout this help center, you'll find embedded Loom video walkthroughs. Korneel records these to show you exactly how to do things, step by step. They're usually 2-5 minutes long and way easier to follow than a wall of text.

If you ever want a custom walkthrough for something specific to your website, just ask on Slack. We're happy to record one for you.
